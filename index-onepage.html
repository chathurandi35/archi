Our Process

  • brief
  • design
  • DESIGN

    DEVELOPMENT

  • DEVELOP THE PRICE
The approach starts with gathering information and requirements for the brief. This allows us to understand the clients expectations in thought with their space requirement, scale, looks, feel, and how they envisage its use and purpose.
The client brief will be answered accordingly to the envision solutions created with the space through idea development, layouts, mood boards and 3D visuals in conformity with their corporate identity or personal taste.
At this stage, we develop the growth of the whole design from the brief, the perception and interrelationships of the whole space and in depth planning that will suit the expectancy of the customer.
Upon the approval of the design by the client, we produces a detailed BOQ and pricing to match the budget and constraints of the client. The comprehensive breakdown of prices ensure transparency between us and the client and allow negotiations for any adjustments.


  • COMMERCIAL

    AGREEMENT

  • CONSTRUCTION

    DRAWINGS

  • FIT-OUT
  • PROJECT

    MANAGEMENT

Once the project has been completely developed, a commercial agreement or commercial contract is established between related personals and the client. With this, the clients can entrust the project with all attached terms and conditions.
We commence the full set of architectural and detailed drawings along with site surveys to ensure that the drawings and all specifications are accurate and are practical in real life situations.
B designs oversees the application of the project and submits on-going and timely reviews and reports from all facets of the team to ensure that targets are achieved.
The Project Management Team assures full responsibility from the minute detailed approvals are provided by the authorities for all on site demolition and construction works, bespoke and off the shelf furniture installation, MEP Co-ordination and full handover of completed site to the client.
  • PROJECT

    HANDOVER

Upon completion of project work, final quality declaration inspections are made prior to handover to the client. Then an overall turning over of the whole project is arranged with the client, including all facets of guarantees, warranties, maintenance, and contact person involved in the whole project.
